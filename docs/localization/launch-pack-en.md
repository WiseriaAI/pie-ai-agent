## Chrome Web Store

### Name

Pie · Open-Source AI Agent for Browser & Tabs

### Short Description

Open-source AI agent for Chrome. Reads pages & PDFs, automates tabs. Bring your own key (11 providers) or subscribe. No telemetry.

### Long Description

Pie is an AI assistant that actually uses your browser — it doesn't just chat. It opens in Chrome's side panel and stays there while you work: tell it what you want in plain language and it gets it done on the page in front of you.

• Ask about any page or PDF you're reading — summaries, key points, answers.
• Hand off multi-step tasks — comparing options, filling forms, gathering info across tabs.
• Tidy up your tabs, search the web, write inside docs and editors, and export results as a file.
• Turn tasks you repeat into one-tap commands, or schedule them to run on their own.

Connect a model your way: bring your own API key (11 providers) — it's free and your data stays on your device — or subscribe to Pie and skip the setup. Either way, Pie collects no tracking or analytics.

Getting started:
1. Install Pie.
2. Open the side panel.
3. Add your key, or sign in to subscribe.
4. Type a task and let Pie work on the page.
